Email Management · Travel Industry

Bringing order to a high-volume international reservation inbox across 3 time zones, with zero lost bookings

At Safari Po Naszemu, the reservations inbox received 80–120 enquiries per week from European and North American clients, many time-sensitive, requiring coordinated responses with hotels, guides and transport providers before clients could be confirmed. Every delayed response is a booking at risk.

What I did
  • I maintain a triage system, urgent (same-day), standard (24hr), low-priority, so response energy was always directed correctly
  • I use 14 templated response types while keeping each message individually personalised to the client's itinerary and preferences
  • I maintain a follow-up tracker in Google Sheets ensuring no enquiry aged beyond agreed response windows
  • I maintain culturally appropriate, professional tone calibrated for EU and NA audiences throughout peak season
Results delivered
<2hrsAverage first-response time maintained throughout peak season
ZeroBookings lost due to missed or delayed communication

Operations · Systems Design

Turning WhatsApp chaos into a structured team operations tracker, built proactively, without being asked

Critical safari updates and team notes were being shared in a high-volume WhatsApp group, making it nearly impossible to track changes, find specific information quickly, or maintain operational clarity during peak season. Key details were getting buried in chat scroll.

What I did
  • I identified the communication breakdown without being prompted, saw the problem before it caused a client-facing failure
  • Built a structured, editable Excel tracker consolidating all active safaris, itinerary notes, status updates and key changes in one shared document
  • Communicated the transition to the team in a way that encouraged immediate adoption without disrupting existing workflow
  • Created the system to be self-maintaining, any team member could update it in real time without training
Results delivered
